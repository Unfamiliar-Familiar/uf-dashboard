Why this works

A retail launch needs three things to go viral on IG: something free to take home, a moment worth photographing, and a cultural anchor that adds depth. Vinyl delivers all three — at a fraction of the cost of any other branded experience.

Unfamiliar Familiar is Dubai's curated record store. We hand-pick records the way Karen curates eyewear — for women who care about how things look, sound, and feel. The aesthetic match is already there.

Three plays for the launch

PLAY 01

Free vinyl for the first 50 visitors

Hand-curated selection (Sade, Olivia Dean, Sabrina Carpenter, Bad Bunny, Lana Del Rey). Each with a small UF tag tying it back to her launch. The freebie everyone takes home and posts about.

Cost: ~AED 2,500 · Reach: ~200-400K story impressions
PLAY 02

The vinyl wall — a photo moment

A styled wall of records inside the store, designed for selfies. Karen's brand colors on the frame, UF branding on the records. Every photo taken in front of it carries both brands.

Cost: ~AED 1,500 setup · One day of fitting
PLAY 03

"KAREN15" — launch-week discount

Her audience gets 15% off curated vinyl crates at UF for the launch week. Continues the relationship after opening day. Easy to track, easy to revoke.

Cost: margin only · Ongoing measurement
