Value Chain
Global Music Distribution — Where UF Sits & Where UF is Going
Artist
Creates the music. Signs with a label.
Label
Owns master rights. Presses vinyl. Licenses territory distribution.
Regional Distributor UF TARGET
Exclusive per territory. Imports, warehouses, and distributes to wholesalers and shops within their region. This is where UF wants to be for UAE/GCC.
Wholesaler / Supplier
Modulor, LAF, EbO — aggregate from distributors, sell to record shops.
Record Shop UF IS HERE
Buys from wholesalers, curates, sells direct to customers. UF's current position.
Customer
End buyer. Vinyl collectors, enthusiasts, gift buyers across UAE.
